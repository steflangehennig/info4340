## Assignment

Choose a real AI incident from the [AI Incident Database](https://incidentdatabase.ai) or another approved source. Write a structured brief.

### Components

1. **Incident summary** — what happened, what system, what context
2. **Failure chain** — technical, human oversight, organizational, and governance failures
3. **Harm analysis** — who was harmed, how severe
4. **Missing evaluation** — what testing or monitoring was absent
5. **Missing governance** — what rules or accountability were absent
6. **Recommended safeguard** — one concrete measure to prevent recurrence
7. **Connection to your work** — could this happen in your domain?

## Rubric

| Criterion | Excellent (5) | Adequate (3) | Needs revision (1) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Incident clarity | Clear, accurate summary with context | Present but missing context | Vague or inaccurate |
| Failure chain | All four layers identified | Some layers identified | Only technical failure |
| Harm analysis | Specific affected populations and severity | Harm mentioned generically | No harm analysis |
| Missing safeguards | Specific evaluation and governance gaps | Some gaps noted | No gap analysis |
| Recommendation | Concrete, actionable, connected to failure chain | Present but generic | Missing |
| Professional connection | Thoughtful application to own domain | Mentioned but thin | No connection |

**Total: 30 points**
