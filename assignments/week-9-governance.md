## Assignment

Write a practical governance memo for your final project's AI workflow.

### Components

1. **Workflow description** — what AI does, inputs, outputs
2. **Risk level** — justified using the Week 1 risk framework
3. **Data rules** — what data enters AI, classification, restrictions
4. **Required evaluation** — test cases, metrics, monitoring
5. **Human review requirements** — who, when, what they check
6. **Disclosure rules** — who needs to know AI was used
7. **Prohibited uses** — what this workflow must not be used for
8. **Accountable owner** — who is responsible
9. **Escalation process** — what happens when something goes wrong

## Rubric

| Criterion | Excellent (5) | Adequate (3) | Needs revision (1) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Workflow clarity | Specific, operational | Present but vague | Missing |
| Risk assessment | Justified with framework | Assigned without justification | No assessment |
| Rules specificity | Specific and implementable | Present but generic | Principles only |
| Evaluation plan | Concrete test cases and metrics | Some evaluation mentioned | No plan |
| Accountability | Clear owner, review chain, escalation | Some accountability | No structure |
| Connection to project | Directly tied to final project | Loosely connected | Disconnected |

**Total: 30 points**
