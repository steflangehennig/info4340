## Assignment

Using the orders dataset from class (or a dataset you choose with instructor approval), produce a directed report with four components.

### Component 1: README and data dictionary

Document the dataset: source and context, unit of observation, variable descriptions (name, type, description, issues, cleaning applied), missingness patterns, and any transformations or computed columns.

You may use AI to draft the data dictionary, but you must verify every entry. Note what the draft got wrong in your AI use log.

### Component 2: Reproducible Python notebook

A notebook or script that runs end-to-end: data import, cleaning steps with comments explaining each decision, aggregation or analysis, at least one visualization, and output generation.

### Component 3: Directed report (1–2 pages)

A short report with four elements:

1. **Question** — a specific, answerable question driving the analysis
2. **Evidence** — descriptive statistics, aggregations, or visualizations tied to the question
3. **Interpretation** — what the evidence means, in context, with appropriate caveats
4. **Limitations** — what the data can't tell you, what assumptions you made

This is not a "dump everything you found" report. Stay focused on your question.

### Component 4: AI use log

Document your AI usage: what prompts you used, what AI produced, what you verified or changed or rejected, and where AI was most helpful vs. misleading.

## Rubric

| Criterion | Excellent (5) | Adequate (3) | Needs revision (1) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Data dictionary | All variables documented accurately with types, descriptions, issues, and cleaning decisions | Most variables documented but some are vague or unverified | Incomplete or clearly AI-generated without verification |
| Code quality | Runs end-to-end, well-commented, cleaning decisions explained | Runs but comments sparse or cleaning logic unclear | Doesn't run or missing major steps |
| Report question | Specific, answerable question with evidence directly tied to it | Question exists but evidence loosely connected | No clear question or vibe-report style |
| Evidence and interpretation | Numbers verified, interpretation contextualizes findings | Some verification but interpretation thin | Numbers not verified or no interpretation |
| Limitations | Identifies specific data limitations and assumptions | Mentions limitations generically | No limitations discussion |
| AI use log | Detailed log with prompts, AI output, and human decisions | Log present but vague | Missing or perfunctory |

**Total: 30 points**
