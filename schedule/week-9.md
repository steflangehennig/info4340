## Learning objectives

1. Explain why GenAI requires organizational governance
2. Identify major governance domains: privacy, security, fairness, transparency, accountability
3. Apply the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage)
4. Draft practical GenAI acceptable-use rules for an analytics team
5. Connect governance requirements to workflow risk levels
