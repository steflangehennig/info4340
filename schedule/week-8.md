## Learning objectives

1. Identify eight common AI failure modes
2. Distinguish technical, human, organizational, and governance failures
3. Analyze a real AI incident using a structured diagnosis worksheet
4. Compute subgroup error rates to identify bias and disparate impact
5. Map failure modes to specific safeguards
